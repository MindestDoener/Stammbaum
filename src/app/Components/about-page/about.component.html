The Vision

We have started building famitry back in early 2021 as a small project. But soon it grew bigger and everyone started to put work into it. As we sat together to clarify our aims everyone should say one quality of the website which is the most important to him. That's where our vision for this project was settled. Simple, fast, free and stylish are the four words which should always describe this website. We all hope that you have the experience we want to deliver. So if you come across any problems using this website feel free to contact us. You can also find quick help on how to use the tree editor down below.

How to?

Create new trees?

  1. navigate to "my trees".
  2. press the button and name the tree as you like
  3. now click the "create" button and you will get to your tree

Delete or rename trees?

  1. navigate to "my trees".
  2. press the button and name the tree as you like or delete it by pressing on
  3. now click the "create" button and you will get to your tree

Export my tree?

  1. navigate to one of your trees
  2. press the button
  3. you can choose between .png file or .json file
  4. if you just like to have a picture of your famitry in your local storage choose the .png file
  5. if you want to save the famitry in your local storage to import it again choose .json file

Import my tree?

  1. navigate to "my trees".
  2. press the button
  3. now select one .json file from your local storage
  4. the tree is now in "my trees" again

Add a person to a tree?

  1. navigate to one of your trees
  2. press the button and fill out at least the required fields marked with a *
  3. now click the "add" button and the person will get added into your tree
  4. if you want to continue adding persons simply check the "Add more" option

Navigate in my tree?

  1. navigate to one of your trees
  2. if you click the button you can always get to the middle of your tree
  3. you can also drag&drop to get to your wished position
  4. or just use the scroll wheel on your mouse to go in or out of the tree

Delete or edit a person?

  1. navigate to one of your trees
  2. double click one of your created persons
  3. now you can see all propertys of the person, just edit them and press the "update" button to save your changes
  4. to delete the person just press the "remove person" button
